What We Are

A platform built on the belief that where you live shapes who you become.

Poiema is the Greek word for a work of art — something carefully and intentionally made. That is how we see every young woman we serve. Our work spans wellness education, policy advocacy, and media, all in service of one idea: that the environments girls live and learn in should be safe, healthy, and worthy of them.
