ποίημα

poiema

Greek noun. A work of art.

Why This Name

In process and already beautiful.

Poiema is a Greek word meaning a work of art, something carefully and intentionally made. In scripture it describes the way God creates people: not as accidents, but as masterworks still being shaped by the master's hand. In process and already beautiful. That is the foundation this work is built on.

Who We Are

A wellness and advocacy platform.

We are in the early stages of building something we believe matters. Poiema Project is a place to teach, reach, and fight for the people who deserve better health information, better environments, and better care.

Our Goal

Close the gap.

To close the gap between the health information people need and what they actually have access to. Through policy, education, and community, one project at a time.
