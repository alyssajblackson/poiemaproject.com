What It Is

For the woman in the middle of figuring it out.

Rooted Rhythm is the publication arm of Poiema Project. It exists for college-aged women who are in the thick of it — navigating health, identity, relationships, ambition, and everything that does not fit neatly into a category.

The name is intentional. Rooted — because the goal is depth, not just surface-level wellness content. Rhythm — because health is not a destination, it is something you build a relationship with over time.

No quick fixes. No unrealistic standards. Just honest conversation from someone who is in it too.

What We Cover

The conversations worth having.

Health

Physical Wellness

Your body deserves honest information — not fear-based content or impossible standards. We cover what it actually feels like to take care of yourself in college.

Mind

Mental Health

The emotional side of college that nobody warns you about. We talk about it directly, without stigma and without scripts.

Identity

Who You Are Becoming

College is one of the few times in life where you get to ask big questions about who you are. We think that deserves space and real conversation.
