Poiema Project is a wellness and advocacy platform. These are the two projects currently underway. More will follow.

What Projects Are

Advocacy and education, one project at a time.

Each project under Poiema Project is built around a specific gap -- a place where people are not getting the information, care, or protection they deserve. Some projects are policy campaigns. Some are publications. Some will be programs, research, or direct work with communities.

Right now there are two. Both are real, both are in progress, and both matter.

01

Policy Campaign / Nonprofit Arm

Clean Dorm Air Act

Alabama ranks 4th in the nation for mold risk and there are zero state laws requiring mold testing in student housing. Students required to live on campus cannot walk away. They pay, they trust, and right now nothing protects them. The Clean Dorm Air Act is a proposed Alabama statute that would require annual independent mold inspection of every dorm before move-in, remediation before occupancy, and post-remediation clearance testing.

Learn More

What We Are Doing

Running an independent mold testing study in Alabama university dorms. Building the evidence base needed to pursue state legislation.

The Goal

Mandatory annual mold testing in every Alabama university dorm before students move in. The first law of its kind in the country.

Where We Are

Study underway. Nonprofit filing in progress. Legislative groundwork being laid for the 2027 Alabama session.

02

Publication

Rooted Rhythm

There is no shortage of health information for college-aged women. But most of it promises clarity while creating more confusion. Rooted Rhythm exists to slow things down -- sifting through the noise, asking better questions, and returning to what is grounded, honest, and life-giving. Health approached through faith, biology, and wisdom.

Learn More

Mission

"Helping women find their God-given rhythm rooted in truth, biology, and scripture."

For college-aged women who want clear answers without overwhelm. Practical steps that work with limited time, money, and cooking access. A trusted voice that blends faith, biology, and truth.
